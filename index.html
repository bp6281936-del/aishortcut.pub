Essential Shortcuts

YouTube

  • K

    Play/Pause the video

  • M

    Mute/Unmute the audio

  • F

    Toggle fullscreen mode

  • J

    Rewind 10 seconds

  • L

    Fast-forward 10 seconds

  • .

    Step to the next frame

  • ,

    Step to the previous frame

Instagram

  • J

    Scroll to the next post

  • K

    Scroll to the previous post

  • L

    Like the current post

  • N

    Open a new post creator

Facebook

  • J

    Scroll to the next post

  • K

    Scroll to the previous post

  • P

    Open photo viewer

  • /

    Open search

  • N

    Create a new post

Twitter (X)

  • J

    Scroll to the next tweet

  • K

    Scroll to the previous tweet

  • L

    Like a tweet

  • N

    Compose a new tweet

  • .

    Load new tweets

Spotify

  • Space

    Play/Pause current song

  • Ctrl+Right

    Next song

  • Ctrl+Left

    Previous song

  • Ctrl+Up

    Increase volume

  • Ctrl+Down

    Decrease volume

Reddit

  • J

    Go to the next post

  • K

    Go to the previous post

  • A

    Upvote a post or comment

  • Z

    Downvote a post or comment

  • C

    Comment on a post

  • R

    Reply to a comment
